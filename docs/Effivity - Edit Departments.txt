Edit Departments

Abstract

This document guides the users to edit departments in Effivity.

Effivity Tech Support
techsupport@effivity.com

Please follow the below instructions to edit Departments. IT is edited as Information Technology in the below example.

Navigate to the Departments Screen under the HR section.

Click on “Edit” option of the department that you would like to edit.

Click on the Org Unit path.

Right click on required department to Edit it.

Enter your new department’s name and save it.

Select any other department and then the Edited Department and click on close to close the pop up. (Unselect and select)

Enter the details such as profile and Department HOD.

Submit the department.

After completing this, the edited department will be available for you in all the screens having department field.

We hope this document helped you!
