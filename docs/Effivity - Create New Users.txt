Create New Users

Abstract

This document guides the users to create new users in Effivity.

Effivity Tech Support
techsupport@effivity.com

Please follow the instructions below to add new users in Effivity.

Navigate to the Employee Screen under the HR section.

Click on “Add New” option.

Fill in the required details.

Check on the “Activate login” check box and submit.

The respective user will receive their login credentials through their mail.
